About Us


Dedicated to celebrating and preserving the rich heritage of Telugu culture, the Telugu Literary Association at VIT Chennai serves as a vibrant hub for Telugu enthusiasts. Our club is committed to organizing diverse events, from literary discussions and workshops to festive celebrations, reflecting the depth and breadth of the Telugu tradition.
