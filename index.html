TLA is a dynamic non-profit organization and college club that celebrates the rich heritage of Telugu literature. Our mission is to promote the beauty and significance of Telugu language and literature among the college community and beyond.

At TLA, we are passionate about fostering a deep appreciation for Telugu literature and its cultural impact. Through a diverse range of activities, events, and initiatives, we provide a platform for enthusiasts and scholars to explore, express, and engage with the essence of Telugu literary works.

As members of TLA, we come together to organize thought-provoking discussions, captivating poetry recitals, literary contests, and immersive storytelling sessions. We believe in nurturing creativity, encouraging critical thinking, and fostering a love for reading and writing in Telugu.
