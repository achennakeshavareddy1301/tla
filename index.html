About TLA


Dedicated to celebrating and preserving the rich heritage of Telugu culture, the Telugu Literary Association at VIT Chennai serves as a vibrant hub for Telugu enthusiasts. Our club is committed to organizing diverse events, from literary discussions and workshops to festive celebrations, reflecting the depth and breadth of the Telugu tradition. Join us as we spread the essence of Telugu culture throughout VIT Chennai University and foster an environment of inclusivity and appreciation. #TeluguCulture #VITChennai #LiteraryEnthusiasm
